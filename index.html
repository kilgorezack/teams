Core Features

Enterprise-grade democracy,

Every feature has been battle-tested in meetings that definitely could have been an email.

Core

Democratic Dismissal™

Any participant can call for a vote to end the meeting at any time. A configurable quorum threshold (default: simple majority) triggers an immediate and binding dismissal. The calendar invite remains, but spiritually, it is over.

  • Configurable quorum: 50%, 66%, or "unanimous except Dave"
  • Countdown timer with dramatic music (optional)
  • Automatic "this could have been an email" post-meeting notification
🚪
Core

Participant Removal Voting

Nominate any participant for removal by majority vote. Reasons are optional but encouraged. "He said 'per my last email'" is a valid reason in 43 U.S. states.

How It Works

From meeting tyranny to
meeting democracy in 3 steps.

Simpler than your org's expense reporting process. Significantly more impactful.

01

Install the App

Add Teams Democracy to your Microsoft Teams tenant in under 3 minutes. IT approval required at most companies. Good luck with that. We provide a pre-written email you can send to your IT department titled "This Is Definitely Not A Security Risk."

→
02

Join Your Meeting

Teams Democracy activates automatically when you join any scheduled meeting. The app silently observes, waits, and watches — like a fair and impartial referee who is absolutely taking notes.

→
03

Exercise Your Rights

When the meeting exceeds its scheduled time, or when the agenda is definitively lost, invoke your democratic rights. Vote to end. Nominate the colleague who has been sharing their screen for 40 minutes and hasn't found the file yet.

🗳️

End Meeting Vote

  1. Any participant clicks "Vote to End"
  2. All participants receive a discreet notification
  3. 60-second voting window opens
  4. If quorum reached: meeting ends with dignity
  5. If quorum missed: everyone knows who didn't vote, and they will live with that
🚪

Removal Vote

  1. Participant nominates a colleague (reason optional)
  2. Nomination is shown to all participants anonymously
  3. 90-second voting window opens
  4. If majority votes yes: participant is removed
  5. Removed participant receives a tasteful "Your presence was not approved" notification
  6. Follow-up calendar invite: "Let's Reconnect (Optional)" — automatically sent
